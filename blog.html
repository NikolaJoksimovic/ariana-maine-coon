maine coon diet,

Ariana 12.07.2023.

Maine Coon cats eat a combination of meat like chicken and fish, dry cat food, and wet cat food. Main Coons are large and energetic, so they need plenty of protein to fuel their play. While they don’t need a specialized diet per se, they need to consume raw or cooked meat. While these cats can get all of their dietary needs through dry and wet cat food, some owners opt for a raw food diet combined with supplements. Stick with high-protein, low-fat meat like poultry, fish, and rabbit. The best raw food diet for Maine Coon cats contains 85% muscle meat, organs, and ground bones. If you choose this diet for your cat, consider adding vitamins and supplements to keep your cat healthy. Veterinary studies confirmed that omega-3 fatty acid supplements help treat inflammatory bowel disease in Maine Coons. Also, don’t forget the cat treats!
